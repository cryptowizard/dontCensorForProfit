Freedom of speach is a Human Right

Freedom of expression is recognized as a human right under article 19 of the Universal Declaration of Human Rights. Some corporations are trying to exert economic pressure on platforms to censor intenet debate. We are asking all people and business to strongly opose those totalitarian aspisrations to limit freedom of indyviduals to express their opinions in a public space.

#DontCensorForPofit

The Freedom of expression and diversity of opinion are values that are necessary for a free and prosperous society. Limiting the individual's right of free expression is the act that only the worst totalitarian regimes have done in the past, always with the grim results.

This campaign was created as a response to #stophateforprofit that is pushing social media sites to implement censorship of content published on those platforms. We believe that the recent actions of large corporations, aimed at exerting economic pressure to limit public debate on social media sites, are a serious threat to those values.

If you do not want to live in the world where those with more money and power are able to shut down the voice of those with less. Please take action and use your voice to express your opposition, if you don’t you may not be able to in the future.
